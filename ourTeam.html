Our Team

On this page you can learn a little bit more about our team behind the magic. To make magic, you need some magicians! Here is our current team behind events and shows wihtin Finland.

Performers

Meet our wonderful performers! Performers, or as many know them, the mermais consists of group of professional speciality divers. >br> All our performers are AIDA or PADI certificate to guarantee the safety of our performances. Our team in specialized in diving and performing in the most peculiar spaces. Our performers are confident performing in water and onland!

Currently "the Mermaid Team" consist of 3 main performers with two on land performers.

Light&Sound

There is no proper show without our light, sound and special effects. Our light crew custom makes light programs to each show according to customers wishes.

Light, sound and special effects team consists of our guru, Juhis, who single handedly produces and monitors all lights and sounds on set.

Logistics

This circus doesn't move anywhere without our logistic team who takes care of transport and builds of each set up. Our logistics team handle everything from transporting our gear, even the biggest ones, to assembling them and when needed tranporting water and handling filtration systems to keep our performers safe.
