Our Team

3Fins Diving offers breathtaking moments underwater! We specialize in underwater entertainment and speciality diving. Our motto is that everything can contain magic!

3Fins diving is a team of underwater performers and diving specialist, logistic and light/sounds specialists who together create performances for all kinds of events.

Performers

Meet our wonderful performers! All our performers have AIDA or PADI certificate in speciality diving. Our team in specialized in diving and performing in the most peculiar spaces. Our performers are a varying team of professionals from dancers to freedivers depending on program number.

Light&Sound

There is no proper show without our light, sound and special effects team. Our light crew custom makes light programs to each show according to customers wishes.

Logistics

This circus doesn't move anywhere without our logisticsteam who take care of transport and buils of each set up.
